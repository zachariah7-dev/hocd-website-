Who We Are

Hope for Orphans and Community Development CBO was formed by community members deeply committed to supporting orphans and vulnerable children in accessing education, care, and essential life skills. We operate at the heart of Kakuma 3, Turkana West — a community where resilience meets compassion.

Registered as a Community-Based Organization serving Turkana County, Sub-County Kakuma.

Our Vision

A Society of Equal Opportunity

A thriving inclusive and self-relaint society where every orphan vulnerable child, youth, women, and family enjoys equal opportunities to learn , grow and live with dignity.

🎯

Our Mission

Empowering Through Action

To improve the quality of life of orphans and vulnerable communities by promoting quality education, youth empowerment, adult literacy, women's empowerment, community health, positive parenting, and sustainable community development through inclusive partnerships and community-driven initiatives.

📍

Our Objectives

What We Work Toward

To improve access to quality education for orphans and vulnerable children.
To empower youth, women, and adults through education, vocational skills, entrepreneurship, and literacy programs.
To promote community health, well-being, and healthy lifestyles.
To strengthen families through positive parenting, child protection, and family support initiatives.
To promote sustainable community development through partnerships, community participation, and livelihood opportunities. To Promote inclusion, equal opportunities, and empowerment for persons with disabilities by supporting their participation in education, community development, and livelihood activities.

📍

Area of Operation

Kakuma 3, Turkana West

Operating in Turkana County, Sub-County Turkana West — Block 16, Zone 1, Kakuma 3. Serving one of Kenya's most underserved refugee and host communities.

What We Do

Programs & Initiatives

Six pillars of work that address the holistic needs of children, families, and communities in Turkana West.

📚

Educational Support for Children

Comprehensive support for school-going children covering fees, materials, tutoring, mentorship, and performance monitoring to keep every child in school.

School Fees Tutoring Mentorship Exam Prep
🛠️

Community Learning & Skills Workshops

Building life-ready skills through literacy, vocational training, entrepreneurship, financial literacy, and peacebuilding workshops for youth and adults.

Life Skills Vocational Entrepreneurship Financial Lit.
🧑‍🎓

Adult Education

Empowering adults with basic literacy, numeracy, digital skills, health education, parenting skills, and livelihood training for economic independence.

Literacy Digital Skills Parenting Health Ed.
💬

Counselling & Guidance

Emotional support, trauma counseling, behavior guidance, family counseling, and peer support groups for children and caregivers facing hardship.

Trauma Support Family Counseling Peer Groups
📢

Child Welfare Awareness Campaigns

Community engagement campaigns on child rights, protection awareness, school attendance drives, back-to-school initiatives, and health education.

Child Rights Back-to-School Health & Hygiene
👪

Positive Parenting Program

Structured parenting support covering positive discipline, emotional wellbeing, child protection, communication skills, and self-care for guardians.

Positive Discipline Child Protection Self-Care
